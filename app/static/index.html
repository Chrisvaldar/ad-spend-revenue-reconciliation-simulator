Ad Spend / Revenue Reconciliation

The matcher tries to link each revenue event to a prior spend by comparing dollar amounts and timing. Example: $100 ad spend might get matched to $97 revenue that shows up 30 seconds later.

Red = spend (money out) Green = revenue (money back)
Matched — spend linked to revenue, confident enough
Pending — spent, still waiting for revenue
Stale — waited too long, no match found
Ambiguous — 2+ spends look good, won't guess which one
Orphan — revenue showed up, can't link it to any spend

What the matcher checks

1. Is revenue amount close enough to a pending spend? (within 15% by default)
2. Did revenue arrive after the spend, within the lookback window?
3. Is confidence above 75%?
4. Is there exactly one clear best candidate? (not ambiguous)
